Oddball Games is a video game development company that seeks to make fun, high quality, games and other software products for a variety of audiences. To further this goal, we are separated into a variety of internal divisions, three of which focuses on different ESRB ratings of video games, while the other two are for experimenting with genres, technology, and other support software.

The Divisions of Oddball Games

PigPen Division

The PigPen Division is geared towards creating fun and interesting games for the newest of people. Thus, all of this division's games intended ESRB ratings of Early Childhood and E for Everyone. Expect to see mainly simplistic looking and educational games being made by this division.

Pigeon Division

The Pigeon Division specializes in games focused on the somewhat older audience. This divisionâ€™s games will be aiming for the ESRB content ratings of E10+ and T for Teen. Expect the games made by this division to more complex and thought challenging that what can be found in the other divisions.

Red Panda Division

The Red Panda Division specializes in games for the discerning adult. Expect the games that this division will be making to have an ESRB content rating of M for Mature and above.

Canary Division

The Canary Division is Oddball Game's experimental video game division. While not making games for a specific audience or category, this division's focus will be to make games for new technology and/or genres that we haven't done before. Expect the games being made by this division to be somewhat shorter than the other divisions, and may include interesting mechanics and gameplay that isn't often found in most games.

Black Bird Division

The Black Bird Division is unique among Oddball Game's divisions. Unlike the other division whose primary focus will be creating fun and interesting video games, this division's primary focus is to develop and support the in-house created software tools that the other divisions use to create their games.
